THE TENANT COVENANTS WITH THE LANDLORD AS FOLLOWS:

1\. To pay the rent reserved in this tenancy agreement at the time and in the manner provided for in this tenancy agreement.

2\. To pay and discharge any charges, bills, taxes, rates, assessment, duties, surcharge and outgoings which are or may be imposed and which are payable now or may be subsequently introduced in respect of the demised apartment during the duration of the tenancy. 

3\. To beautify and redecorate the demised apartment at his own expenses and cost. Such redecoration shall be remitted to the Landlord at the determination of the tenancy at no cost. 

4\. Not to do or cause to be done on the Demised apartment or any part thereof any act or thing whatsoever which may be or tend to be of any nuisance, damage or unreasonable disturbance to the Landlord, other tenants in the property or other occupiers of any adjoining or neighboring properties. 

5\. Not to do or permit to be done any structural alteration to or addition to the premises or any part of it without first obtaining the Landlord's written consent; such consent not to be unreasonably withheld. 

6\. Not to assign, lease, sublet, charge, grant any security, underlet or share or part with possession or occupation of the apartment or any part of it without the written consent of the landlord; such consent not to be unreasonably withheld or delayed in case of a responsible and respectable person. 

7\. Upon the expiration of his rent, to pay for the renewal of rent, within one (1) week of the expiration of the last payment, subject however to such rent as may be agreed to by the parties. 

8\. To allow the Landlord, his agents and/or workmen at all reasonable time during the term granted to enter upon and inspect the apartment and effect repairs, when necessary, upon giving forty-eight (48) hours' notice. 

9\. Not to store or bring upon the demised apartment, any article, mineral of combustible, inflammable or dangerous nature.

10\. To indemnify the Landlord in full for any and all claims, liabilities, losses and or damages suffered by the Landlord which are attributable to the tenant's non-performance or non-observance of its covenants herein. 

11\. To keep the demised apartment in good tenantable condition and repair, fair wear and tear accepted and at the expiration of the tenancy to deliver the apartment in the same condition as it were at the commencement of the tenancy. 

THE LANDLORD COVENANTS WITH THE TENANT AS FOLLOWS: 

The Tenant paying the rent and observing all the covenants contained in the tenancy agreement, may quietly and peacefully hold and enjoy the demised property during the subsistence of his tenancy without any interruption or disturbance by the landlord or any person claiming through or in trust for him. 

IT IS FURTHER AGREED BETWEEN THE PARTIES THAT: 

a) The rent reserved in this tenancy agreement shall be reviewable at such a time as may be determined by the landlord. Provided that the landlord or any person claiming through him shall give notice of such reviewed rent, at least one month to the expiration of the current term. 

b) That if the reviewed-rent hereby agreed to or any part thereof shall remain unpaid for one month after becoming due and payable, whether formally demanded or not, or if any covenant on the part of the tenant herein contained shall not be observed, it shall be lawful for the Landlord or his agents at any time thereafter to re-enter and re-possess the apartment or any part thereof, and thereupon, this tenancy shall be absolutely determined but without prejudice to the right of action of the Landlord in respect of the breach of covenant on the part of the tenant herein contained. 

c) Any notice required to be served under this Tenancy Agreement shall be deemed to be properly served if forwarded to the parties' address above or such other place as the parties may from time to time notify each other in writing.

d) This tenancy agreement represents the entire agreement between the parties. No variation of this agreement shall be valid or effective unless and until it is made in writing and duly executed by the parties hereto. 

e) This Tenancy Agreement shall be governed and construed in accordance with the Laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria and any dispute arising therefrom shall be determined in accordance with such laws for the time being in force.
